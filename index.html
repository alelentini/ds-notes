About

  • These notes tries to give a high-level view of data science.
  • A practical definition is given highlighting its relation to the disciplines that already exists and solve the same problem.
  • Focus is on overall processes, methodologies and frameworks, showing how data science is useful to solve certain kind of problem.
  • Comprehensive technical understanding of concepts and techniques will require further in-depth reading.
